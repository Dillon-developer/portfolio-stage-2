Intro: How the journey began

Hello, I am from San Antonio, Texas. I started school with a Science major. I completed a four-year degree but I was not happy with jobs in my field. This was my opening into the world, but it was merely a stepping stone.

The next venture I jumped into was Digital Marketing. This is where I learned all about business, risk, and return. I would say that digital marketing was a very thrilling puzzle that had to be adjusted with every industry and business owner.
